## Login Instructions

1. Go to the login page
2. Select your role (Administrator, Librarian, or Citizen/Reader)
3. Enter your email address
4. Enter your password
5. Click "Sign In"
6. You will be redirected to the appropriate dashboard based on your role

---

## Role-Based Access

### Administrator Dashboard
- System Settings (fine rates, borrowing limits, membership fees)
- Library Management (add/delete libraries)
- Approve/Reject borrow requests
- View all transactions and history
- Reports & Analytics
- Fees Management

### Librarian Dashboard
- Library-specific settings (operating hours, capacity)
- Circulation Management (issue/return books)
- Reader Check-in/Check-out
- Borrow Request approvals for their library
- Transaction history
- Fees Management for their library

### Citizen Dashboard
- Search and browse books
- Make borrow requests
- View notifications (approval/rejection status)
- View borrowed books
- View personal history
- Pay fines
- Register for events

---

## Notes

- All passwords are case-sensitive
- Emails must match exactly (including domain)
- Citizens can register new accounts via the "Register as Citizen" link
- Each librarian is assigned to a specific library branch
- Admin has access to all libraries and system-wide settings
